What is this?

Founding a successful company is undoubtedly one of the toughest things an individual could choose as a professional career. Join us in The Founder Stories track where 8 hand-picked promising technology startup founders will give you unique insights of what they've had to go through before getting a "promising" badge. You will find us at the Node5 Stage, WebExpo Conference.

Location: Webexpo conference, Node 5 Stage
